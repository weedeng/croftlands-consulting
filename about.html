About Croftlands Consulting

Croftlands Consulting provides fractional CFO, interim management, and strategic finance execution—giving scale-ups and mid-sized businesses senior financial leadership without full-time overhead.

The Practice

Senior Finance Leadership, Built for the AI Era

Croftlands Consulting delivers interim, fractional, and project CFO leadership to high-growth SMEs and PE-backed businesses. Founded in 2010, we bring institutional-grade financial control and strategic rigour to founder-led scale-ups—with deep expertise across SaaS, fintech, events, financial services, and complex multi-entity structures.

The premise is simple: growth businesses need CFO-level financial leadership long before they can justify a full-time executive hire—and today, they need a finance function that actively executes on AI and machine learning, rather than just talking about it. Croftlands delivers both: the board-level judgment of a career CFO, and the hands-on data science to build predictive models, automate reporting, and execute AI transformation. One accountable partner on both sides of the table.

Every engagement is delivered personally by the founder—augmented by modern AI tooling, with zero delegation to junior consultants or offshore teams. What you scope is what you get.

Why Croftlands

What Makes the Practice Different

Where board-level CFO judgment meets hands-on data science. Croftlands bridges the gap between high-level financial strategy and modern AI execution.

Seasoned Operator, Not a Consultant

25 years of boardroom leadership as a hands-on CFO. CA(SA) and Big 4 trained, with deep experience in finance build-outs, statutory audits, and multi-jurisdictional M&A. The battle-tested judgment that only comes from running finance functions under real commercial pressure.

CFO Judgment + Applied AI

MIT-trained capability across Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and generative AI. From predictive churn scoring to automated commentary, every model is built by a CFO who understands both the underlying mathematics and the balance sheet—bringing pragmatic honesty to what AI can and cannot solve.

The Practice Runs on AI

Croftlands does not just advise on AI—it operates on it. Client deliverables, financial pipelines, and analytical tools are engineered directly using cutting-edge LLMs and modern enterprise AI APIs. The result: rapid execution, lower delivery costs, and intelligent systems that integrate seamlessly into your business.

100% White-Box & Accountable

One senior partner owns the engagement from diagnostic to handover. No black-box algorithms: every financial model, forecast, and automated narrative is fully auditable, transparent, and explainable to founders, board members, and PE investors alike.

How We Work

Diagnostic First, Then Tailored Delivery

Every engagement starts by identifying the primary strategic driver—whether that is steady-state reporting discipline, a live fundraise or exit, a finance transformation, or interim executive cover—and structuring the scope around it. Nothing gets rebuilt until the real starting position is thoroughly audited from raw source data rather than static dashboards.

From there, most interim and fractional engagements follow a structured 100-day plan: establish the baseline, build a reliable reporting and forecasting spine, deploy the strategic AI layer, and prove the new operating rhythm works before handing over full documentation to eliminate key-person risk.
