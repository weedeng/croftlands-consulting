About Croftlands Consulting

A boutique interim and fractional CFO practice for PE-backed and founder-led SMEs — combining 25 years of board-level finance leadership with MIT-trained machine learning, deployed on every engagement.

The Practice

Senior Finance Leadership, Built for the AI Age

Croftlands Consulting is a specialist finance practice providing interim CFO, fractional CFO and project-based finance leadership to small and mid-sized businesses. Founded in 2010, it works predominantly with PE-backed and founder-led companies — with particular depth in SaaS, events and financial services, and broad experience across professional services and multi-entity groups.

The premise is simple. Growth businesses need CFO-level financial rigour long before they can justify a full-time hire — and, increasingly, they need a finance function that uses machine learning and AI rather than talking about it. Croftlands delivers both: the board-level judgment of a career CFO, and the hands-on data science to build churn models, automate reporting and run an AI transformation. One accountable person, both sides of the table.

Every engagement is delivered personally by the founder, augmented by AI tooling — never staffed down to junior consultants or handed to an offshore team. What you scope is what you get.

Why Croftlands

What Makes the Practice Different

Most finance consultants offer one of two things: a safe pair of hands, or a data team that does not understand finance. Croftlands is built on the combination.

Career CFO, Not a Generalist

25 years at board and executive level — interim CFO, finance build-outs, statutory audits, transfer pricing and multi-jurisdiction acquisition integration. CA(SA), Big 4 trained. The judgment that comes from having actually done it, repeatedly, under pressure.

Finance and AI in One Person

MIT-trained, hands-on machine learning capability — churn prediction, customer segmentation, forecasting and generative-AI reporting — applied by a CFO who understands both the accounting and the models, and will be honest about which problems AI actually solves.

The Practice Runs on AI

Croftlands does not just advise on AI adoption — it runs on it. Models, automations and client deliverables are built and maintained using Claude Code and the Anthropic API, so turnaround is faster than traditional consulting and the tools are genuinely explainable to a board.

Accountable and Explainable

One senior person owns the outcome from diagnostic to handover. Every model and every forecast can be explained — how the number was calculated, what drives it, and what its limits are — to a founder, a board and a PE investor alike.

How We Work

Diagnostic First, Then Tailored Delivery

Every engagement starts by naming the actual driver — steady-state reporting discipline, a live fundraise or sale, a finance transformation, or cover for a departure — and weighting the work around it. Nothing gets rebuilt until the real starting position is understood from source data rather than dashboards.

From there, most interim and fractional engagements follow a structured 100-day plan: understand the baseline, build a reliable reporting and forecasting spine, add the strategic and external-facing layer, and prove the new operating rhythm works before handing over documentation that removes key-person risk.
