Profile

Senior Finance Leader, CFO Advisor & Applied Data Scientist

Graeme Weeden is a Chartered Accountant (South Africa) with over 25 years of experience operating at board and executive level across private equity-backed, acquisitive and complex multi-entity businesses in the United Kingdom and South Africa.

His career began at KPMG in Cape Town, where he qualified as a Chartered Accountant before moving to London and building a decade of experience in investment banking — as a product controller and treasury risk manager at Lehman Brothers, UBS Warburg, CSFB and EBRD — before returning to South Africa to take on C-suite roles at ABN AMRO and Coronation Capital.

Since 2010, Graeme has operated primarily as a finance consultant and interim CFO to SMEs and growth businesses, with recent senior engagements at Dext Software (global PE-backed SaaS), Informa plc (FTSE 100) and Raccoon Media Group (PE-backed international events).

Graeme is a graduate of the MIT Professional Education Applied AI & Data Science Program (2026 cohort, 8 courses, 61 working models) — applying machine learning techniques including XGBoost churn prediction, ARIMA and SARIMAX time-series forecasting, K-Means and hierarchical customer segmentation, BERT-based NLP, and LLM pipeline automation (Claude API, OpenAI GPT) directly to client mandates. This is not a credential held at arm’s length — it is hands-on technical capability built alongside client work and deployed in practice.
