Machine Learning Applied to Your Finance Function

Three capabilities drawn from the MIT Applied AI & Data Science Program — deployed directly in client engagements, not as a data science experiment, but by a CFO who knows what the output means for the business. Whether you need a single model built or your whole finance function transformed, Graeme can run the programme hands-on or oversee an existing team.

Predictive Analytics

XGBoost · Random Forest · Logistic Regression

Churn risk scoring that identifies at-risk SaaS customers weeks before cancellation. Attrition prediction for PE-backed businesses. Lead conversion probability scoring from CRM data. Classification models that turn historical patterns into actionable probabilities per customer or account.

Customer Intelligence

K-Means · Hierarchical Clustering · PCA / t-SNE

Customer segmentation that reveals natural groupings in your base — by behaviour, contract value, product usage or acquisition channel. Used to drive pricing strategy, upsell targeting, cross-sell recommendations and contract renewal prioritisation. Visualised using PCA and t-SNE for board-level presentation.

Generative AI & Automation

Claude API · OpenAI GPT · LLM Pipelines

Automated board pack commentary, variance narrative generation and document summarisation using the Claude and OpenAI APIs. Finance workflows re-engineered around LLM pipelines so management reports are generated from actuals data overnight — with zero-shot and few-shot prompting calibrated to your reporting style.

About Graeme Weeden

25 Years of Finance Leadership — With a Machine Learning Edge

Graeme Weeden is a CA(SA)-qualified finance leader with over 25 years of board and C-suite experience across private equity-backed businesses, investment banking, and independent consulting in South Africa and the United Kingdom.

His career spans KPMG (articled clerk through to audit supervisor), investment banking in London (Lehman Brothers, UBS Warburg, CSFB), ABN AMRO, Coronation Capital (COO), and senior interim roles at Dext Software, Informa plc and Raccoon Media Group.

What sets Graeme apart is the combination of deep CFO credentials with hands-on machine learning capability — XGBoost churn models, K-Means segmentation and LLM-powered reporting pipelines, deployed directly in client engagements as a graduate of the MIT Applied AI & Data Science Program (8 courses, 61 working models).
