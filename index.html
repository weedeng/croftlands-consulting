About Graeme Weeden

25 Years of Finance Leadership — With a Data Science Edge

Graeme Weeden is a CA(SA)-qualified finance leader with over 25 years of board and C-suite experience across private equity-backed businesses, investment banking, and independent consulting in South Africa and the United Kingdom.

His career spans KPMG (articled clerk through to audit supervisor), investment banking in London (Lehman Brothers, UBS Warburg, CSFB), ABN AMRO, Coronation Capital (COO), and senior interim roles at Dext Software, Informa plc and Raccoon Media Group.

What sets Graeme apart is the combination of deep CFO credentials with genuine technical capability in Python-driven data analytics — actively studying in the MIT Professional Education Applied AI & Data Science Program alongside client work.
