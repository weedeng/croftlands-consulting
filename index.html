Machine Learning & AI Applied to Your P&L

Three core capabilities drawn from the MIT Applied AI & Data Science Program—deployed directly in client engagements by a CFO who knows what the numbers mean for the business. Whether you need a single predictive model built or a full AI transformation, Croftlands delivers hands-on execution or technical governance.

Predictive Analytics

XGBoost · Random Forest · Deep Learning

Predictive churn risk scoring that identifies at-risk SaaS accounts weeks before cancellation. Attrition prediction for PE-backed businesses, lead conversion probability scoring from CRM data, and classification models that turn historical patterns into actionable probabilities per account.

Customer Intelligence

Customer segmentation that reveals natural behavioral groupings across your base. Used to drive pricing tier strategy, upsell targeting, cross-sell recommendations, and contract renewal prioritization—visualized cleanly for board-level presentation.

About Graeme Weeden

25 Years of Finance Leadership — With a Machine Learning Edge

Graeme Weeden is a CA(SA)-qualified finance leader with over 25 years of board and C-suite experience across private equity-backed businesses, investment banking, and independent consulting in South Africa and the United Kingdom.

His career spans KPMG (articled clerk through to audit supervisor), investment banking in London (Lehman Brothers, UBS Warburg, CSFB), ABN AMRO, Coronation Capital (COO), and senior interim roles at Dext Software, Informa plc, and Raccoon Media Group.

What sets Graeme apart is combining senior CFO credentials with hands-on machine learning capability—XGBoost churn models, K-Means segmentation, and LLM-powered reporting pipelines, deployed directly in client mandates as a graduate of the MIT Applied AI & Data Science Program (8 courses, 61 working models).
