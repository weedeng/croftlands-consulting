What to Expect

The Discovery Call

A typical first conversation lasts 30 to 45 minutes. There’s no pitch and no obligation. The goal is to understand your business, the specific challenge or requirement, and whether Croftlands Consulting is the right fit.

If it’s a good match, we’ll agree a scope of work and a day rate or project fee. Engagements can typically start within one to two weeks of agreeing terms.

Typical Engagement Scenarios

“Our CFO left suddenly and we need someone in immediately.”

“We’re closing an acquisition and need someone to run the integration.”

“We’re a SaaS business and we don’t really trust our financial numbers.”

“We’re raising a round and need investor-grade financial reporting.”

“I want a part-time CFO who will actually engage with the business, not just review spreadsheets.”

“We have a cashflow problem and need someone who can take ownership of it this week.”
